GODZILLA: King of The Monsters

SYNOPSIS

Bad people want to release big dragon. Big dragon wakes up other big monsters/titans and destroy cities. Good guys find giant lizard to fight big dragon



CHARACTERS




1. GODZILLA

One of the main characters of the movie. He is the king of the monsters, only rivaled by Monster Zero. He keeps the balance of the earth by checking on the other titans, preventing them from rogue. He has dark grey, rough skin, dorsal plates on his back. Godzilla has the ability to release an energy beam than can burn through almost anything, it is known as the ATOMIC BREATH.




2. KING GHIDORAH

One of the main antagonists of the movie. The rival to Godzilla, Monster Zero. King Ghidorah has 3 heads, each with their own personality. Ghidorah has golden skin and wings that dwarf even the mighty Godzilla. Similiarly to his rival, King Ghidorah can shoot lighting-like beams known as GRAVITY BEAMS.




3. MOTHRA

Mothra, queen of the monsters. Mothra can release a spider-like web to immobilize her enemies. Mothra can also create 'God Rays' that blind her anyone near her. She can also tranfer her energy to other titans when she dies. But, she won't be dead forever, as she can rebirth herself by laying an egg in a secret location and hatching it when her body dies, creating a new Mothra.




4. RODAN

Rodan, also known as the FIRE DEMON, was found hibernating in a volcano. His skin is covered in what looks to be lava that has cooled. He doesn't have much abilities, but what he does have is a giant wingspan that he can use to destroy entire cities simply by flying over them.
